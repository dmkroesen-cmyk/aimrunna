# AImRUNNA — Training Methodology Reference (2024-2025)

Internal synthesis for the plan-engine. Evidence-based principles with peer-reviewed sources.
Last updated: 2026-04-05

---

## 1. Norwegian Method (Seiler, Olbrecht, Ingebrigtsen)

**Core:** High-volume, lactate-guided, sub-threshold training. Double-threshold days (AM+PM),
controlled lactate (2.5-3.5 mmol/L = LT1-to-LT2), sparse VO2max (1×/10-14d), 85-90% Z1.

**Amateur adaptation:** NOT suitable <50-60 km/wk. Collapse to single-threshold 2×/wk with
RPE cap ("comfortably hard, could do more").

**Sources:** Seiler 2010 (*IJSPP*), Tjelta 2019 (*IJSSC*), Haugen et al. 2022 (*Sports Med Open*),
Casado et al. 2022 (*IJSPP*).

---

## 2. Polarized vs Pyramidal vs Threshold

| Distribution | Z1 | Z2 | Z3 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Polarized (POL) | ~80% | ~0-5% | ~15-20% |
| Pyramidal (PYR) | ~80% | ~15% | ~5% |
| Threshold (THR) | ~45% | ~50% | ~5% |

**Consensus 2024-2025:** Rosenblat 2022 meta-analysis found no significant difference POL vs PYR
when total load matched. Base = pyramidal, pre-comp = polarized, race-specific threshold for
half/marathon.

**Guardrail:** Never exceed ~15% Z3 in any microcycle.

**Sources:** Seiler & Kjerland 2006, Stöggl & Sperlich 2014, Rosenblat et al. 2022 (*Sports Med*),
Kenneally et al. 2018 (*IJSPP*).

## 3. Periodization

**Block (Issurin):** Residual training effects — aerobic ~30d, max strength ~30d, glycolytic ~18d,
speed ~5d. Accumulation → Transmutation → Realization.

**Amateur rules:**
- <6 mo training age → linear, 3-4wk load + 1wk deload
- Intermediate → block, 3wk load + 1wk deload
- Advanced/time-crunched → micro-blocks 1-2wk

**Guardrails:** Volume ramp ≤10%/wk (Gabbett 2016), ACWR 0.8-1.3, deload every 3rd-4th week.

**Sources:** Issurin 2010, Kiely 2018, Mujika et al. 2018.

---

## 4. Concurrent Training (Run + Strength + HYROX)

**Interference effect (Hickson 1980):** Strength/hypertrophy impaired by concurrent endurance;
endurance largely preserved.

**Optimal sequencing:**
- Same day: ≥6h gap between strength and endurance
- Never stack heavy lower-body lift + long run same day (intermediates)
- Upper-body lifting pairs freely with run days

**Volume limits:** ≤10 total sessions/wk for non-elite hybrid, ≤3 hard days/wk.

**Sources:** Wilson et al. 2012, Schumann et al. 2022, Robineau et al. 2016, Coffey & Hawley 2017.

---

## 5. HYROX-Specific Training

**Race:** 8×1km run + 8 stations (SkiErg, SledPush/Pull, BurpeeBroadJump, Row, Farmers, SandbagLunges, WallBalls). ~85-90% HRmax sustained.

**Weekly volume by level:**
- Beginner: 20-30 km/wk + 2 strength
- Intermediate: 35-50 km/wk + 2-3 strength
- Advanced: 50-70 km/wk + 2-3 strength

**Mandatory:** ≥1 compromised-running session/wk (run→station→run brick), weekly station work.

**Literature note:** Peer-reviewed HYROX research is nascent (2023-2025 emerging).

---

## 6. Cycling FTP Building

- **Time-crunched (<6 hr/wk):** Sweet Spot (88-94% FTP) + 1 VO2max weekly
- **High volume (>10 hr/wk):** Polarized (80/20)

**Sources:** Rønnestad et al. 2020, Hebisz et al. 2022, Muriel et al. 2022.

---

## 7. Tapering (Mujika Framework)

| Event | Duration | Volume Reduction |
|---|---|---|
| 5K-10K | 7-10d | 40-50% |
| Half marathon | 10-14d | 50-60% |
| Marathon | 14-21d | 50-60% |
| HYROX | 10-14d | 50% |
| Ironman | 21-28d | 50-60% |

**Rules:** Maintain intensity, cut volume exponentially, slight frequency reduction (≤20%).
Expected gain: 1-3% TT performance.

**Sources:** Mujika & Padilla 2003, Bosquet et al. 2007 meta-analysis.

---

## 8. Athlete Level Guardrails

**Beginner (<6mo):** 3-4 sessions/wk, Z1-only first 12 weeks, ≤30-35 km/wk ceiling, CTL ramp ≤5 TSS/wk.

**Intermediate (6mo-3y):** 4-6 sessions/wk, 2-3 quality/wk, 40-70 km/wk, CTL ramp ≤8 TSS/wk.

**Advanced (3y+):** 6-12 sessions/wk, doubles viable, 70-130+ km/wk, individualized zones.

**Z1/Z2/Z3 distribution by phase:**
| Phase | Z1 | Z2 | Z3 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Base | 85-90% | 5-10% | 0-5% |
| Build | 75-80% | 10-15% | 8-12% |
| Peak | 75-80% | 5-10% | 12-18% |
| Taper | 70-80% | 10% | 10-15% |

## Plan-Engine Enforcement Checklist

1. Intensity distribution ±5% of chosen template (POL/PYR/THR)
2. Hard-day cap: ≤3 Z3/wk, never consecutive
3. ACWR check: reject weeks with projected 0.8 < ACWR > 1.3
4. Volume ramp ≤10%
5. Mandatory deload every 3rd-4th week (-30-50%)
6. Concurrent spacing ≥6h; forbid heavy-lower+long-run same day
7. Taper auto-gen by event distance (§7)
8. Beginners locked out of Z3 first 8-12 weeks (strides only)
9. HYROX: ≥1 compromised session + weekly station work
10. Cycling ≤6h/wk → sweet spot; >10h/wk → polarized
